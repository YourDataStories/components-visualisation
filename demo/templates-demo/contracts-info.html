YourDataStories: Modelling TED data

YourDataStories looks at TED broken down into individual “Contracts”. A Contract has:

  • a Buyer (Contracting Authority) and a Seller (Contractor),
  • a duration or date range,
  • a price,
  • a CPV (Common Procurement Vocabulary) (e.g. Construction work for highways, Air-sea rescue services), a CPV Code

A quick overview of the Dashboard

The two maps show all buyers and sellers of the selected countries that are available in the data, coloured according to the number of Contracts they have been involved in. Both maps are interactive, allowing the selection of any number and combination of countries.

In the next step, a slider allows the selection of any time period within the available data.

Finally, there is a set of "vertical tabs" that allow you to filter for various aspects of the TED data. Clicking on each "vertical tab" shows predefined visualisations. Using the icons in the upper right corner of the visualisation, you can toggle between various types of infographics. The icons in the upper left corner allow you to generate an embed code to use the visualisation on another website, and to add it to your personal data collection ("library").
